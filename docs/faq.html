FAQs

General

The opening of roles will occur towards the end of Semester 2.

After submitting your application, you will be interviewed by the current EXCOs. This interview will evaluate your suitability for the role.

We will be looking for individuals who are passionate about creating a difference in the Fintech space.

The interview will consist of both technical and non-technical components.

For non-technical components, we will assess your critical thinking skills and passion for the Fintech space.

Our Value Proposition

As we are the first Fintech club established in NTU, we offer a rare opportunity to bridge the gap between academic learning and the rapidly evolving world of financial technology.

You'll gain hands-on experience through workshops, events, and projects designed to simulate real-world Fintech challenges.

Additionally, our strong network with industry professionals and alumni provides invaluable insights and mentorship.

By joining, you'll develop a comprehensive understanding of Fintech trends and innovations while building technical and soft skills essential for your career.

You’ll have access to exclusive learning resources, networking events, and collaboration opportunities with like-minded peers, all of which position you to excel in the dynamic Fintech industry.

This role offers a platform to enhance leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities in a fast-paced environment.

You’ll gain practical exposure to Fintech tools and concepts, improving your technical expertise while also honing your communication and organizational skills.

Additionally, by working closely with peers and industry leaders, you’ll expand your professional network and prepare for a successful career in Fintech.
